The Starter Discovery Package is designed to introduce students to the fascinating world of artificial intelligence in a structured, beginner-friendly format. This package provides a solid foundation in core AI concepts, making it ideal for those just starting their AI learning journey.

Duration and Structure

This package includes 10 one-on-one sessions, each lasting 1 hour. The personalized approach ensures that students receive focused attention and can progress at their own pace.

Features

  • Students will learn core AI concepts through an introductory curriculum that covers the basics of artificial intelligence.
  • Comprehensive learning materials are provided to support students during and between sessions, ensuring they have the resources needed to reinforce their understanding.
  • Basic support is available between sessions to address any questions or challenges, helping students stay on track.

Highlights

  • Engaging assessments and quizzes designed to reinforce the concepts covered in each session.
  • Students earn digital badges after each session, celebrating their progress and accomplishments. These badges serve as motivating milestones throughout the program.

Add-On Options

Students can opt for additional support with 30-minute Q&A follow-up sessions at a cost of USD 18 each. Up to 3 of these add-on sessions are available, providing an excellent opportunity to dive deeper into specific topics or clarify doubts.

Why Choose the Starter Discovery Package?

This package is perfect for students who are curious about AI but may have little to no prior experience. It emphasizes a fun and supportive learning environment, helping participants build confidence while mastering foundational AI concepts. With a combination of structured lessons, engaging activities, and personalized support, the Starter Discovery Package offers an excellent starting point for exploring the exciting world of artificial intelligence.
