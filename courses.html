AI Essential for Work

In today’s fast-paced workplace, AI is more than just a trend—it’s a powerful tool for boosting productivity. "ChatGPT: AI Essential for Work" is a hands-on course designed to help office professionals unlock the full potential of ChatGPT to streamline daily tasks, generate ideas, and improve communication. Whether it’s writing emails, summarizing documents, or solving work-related problems, this beginnerfriendly course provides practical skills to integrate AI into your workflow effortlessly. No technical background required—just real-world applications that help you work smarter, not harder.

AI Adventure for Kids

Designed for kids aged 8-16, AI Adventure for Kids is a fun and interactive 4-session course that introduces young learners to the exciting world of artificial intelligence through ChatGPT. Kids will explore AI concepts, generate creative ideas, design games, and create digital images— all while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills. With hands-on activities and bite-sized lessons, this course makes AI learning engaging, age-appropriate, and ethically responsible.
