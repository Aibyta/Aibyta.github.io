The Junior Innovation Package is designed to introduce young learners to artificial intelligence and machine learning in a way that is engaging, practical, and inspiring. This package helps students build a strong foundation in AI concepts while fostering creativity, problem-solving, and confidence through hands-on projects and real-world applications.

Duration and Structure

The program includes 10 personalized one-on-one sessions, each lasting 1 hour. This format ensures that students receive focused attention and can progress at a pace that suits their individual learning needs.

Features

  • Covers AI and machine learning basics, introducing students to foundational concepts and principles in an easy-to-understand manner.
  • Includes two mini-projects that allow students to apply their knowledge to practical tasks, fostering a deeper understanding of AI's potential in everyday scenarios.
  • Emphasizes real-world applications of AI, showing how these technologies are used to solve problems and improve lives globally.
  • Regular skills assessments are conducted to track progress, helping identify areas for improvement and ensuring a thorough understanding of the material.

Highlights

  • A balanced curriculum integrates structured lessons with creative activities, ensuring that learning remains engaging and fun.
  • Parent involvement is encouraged through parent-child "review sessions" after every five sessions, promoting collaboration and a shared understanding of the childâ€™s progress.
  • Students earn digital badges after each session, recognizing their achievements and motivating them to continue learning with enthusiasm.

Extras

  • Includes two free workshop invitations, giving students opportunities to explore specialized AI topics, collaborate with peers, and gain additional insights.
  • Provides ongoing email support for both students and parents, ensuring timely assistance with questions or challenges throughout the program.

Add-On Options

Personalized projects are available for an additional cost of USD 40. These projects allow students to explore specific interests, tackle advanced challenges, and showcase their creativity and technical skills.

Why Choose the Junior Innovation Package?

This package is perfect for students who are curious about AI and machine learning and want to explore its potential in a fun, interactive, and supportive environment. With a focus on hands-on learning, real-world applications, and collaborative progress tracking, the Junior Innovation Package provides young learners with a strong foundation and the confidence to continue their journey in the exciting field of AI.
