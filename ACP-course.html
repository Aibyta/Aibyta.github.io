Advance Creator Package

Book a Trial     Join Now

The Advance Creator Package is tailored for students who are ready to explore in-depth AI concepts and work on structured, high-impact projects. This program is perfect for those who aim to develop advanced skills and foster independent learning.

Duration and Structure

The program includes 10 personalized one-on-one sessions, each lasting 1 hour, plus two supportive sessions (30 minutes each) for homework assistance, ensuring a comprehensive and well-rounded learning experience.

Features

  • Covers in-depth AI concepts, providing a deep dive into advanced topics that equip students with the knowledge to design and implement AI systems.
  • Students complete four structured projects during the program, culminating in a student presentation of their work. These projects are designed to demonstrate real-world applications of AI, such as recommendation systems, predictive models, or creative AI tools.
  • Completed projects are showcased in academy bulletins headlines, offering students recognition and a platform to display their skills and creativity.

Highlights

  • The program emphasizes creative outlets and independent learning, encouraging students to think critically, solve problems, and innovate using AI.
  • Highly engaging sessions include exclusive learning experiences designed to make the program both enjoyable and intellectually stimulating.

Extras

  • Includes two free workshop invitations, where students can explore advanced topics, interact with peers, and deepen their understanding of AI.
  • Provides ongoing email support, ensuring students and parents receive timely assistance with any questions or challenges.
  • Features parent-child "review sessions" after every five sessions, offering collaborative progress tracking and personalized feedback.

Digital Rewards

  • Students earn digital badges after each session, celebrating their progress and motivating continued learning.

Add-On Options

Students can opt for personalized projects at an additional cost of USD 40. These projects allow them to delve into specific areas of interest, tackle unique challenges, and demonstrate their creativity and technical expertise.

Why Choose the Advance Creator Package?

By combining in-depth learning, structured projects, and opportunities for recognition, this program equips learners with the tools and confidence to excel in AI. With its emphasis on creativity, problem-solving, and real-world applications, this package ensures a transformative experience that inspires innovation and prepares students for future challenges in artificial intelligence.
