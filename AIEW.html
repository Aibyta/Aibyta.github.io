Course Overview:

"ChatGPT: AI Essential for Work" is a practical, hands-on course designed to introduce office workers to the power of ChatGPT and how it can be used to enhance productivity in the workplace. This beginner-friendly course explains the world of artificial intelligence and equips participants with the skills to use ChatGPT effectively for everyday tasks like writing emails,summarizing documents, generating ideas, and solving basic work-related problems.Participants will learn how to interact with ChatGPT beyond simple Q&A, leveraging its capabilities to streamline their workflow, increase efficiency, and improve communication. By the end of the course, participants will feel confident using ChatGPT as a valuable tool in their day-to-day work, without needing any prior technical knowledge.

What You'll Learn:

  • Introduction to ChatGPT: Understand what ChatGPT is and how it can assist with language-based tasks.
  • Basic Functions: Learn how to ask questions, generate responses, and perform basic tasks like drafting emails and summarizing documents.
  • Enhancing Productivity: Discover how ChatGPT can be used to automate routine tasks,generate creative ideas, and support decision-making.
  • Crafting Effective Prompts: Develop skills for writing clear and effective prompts to get the best responses from ChatGPT.
  • Hands-On Practice: Apply your knowledge in real-time with guided exercises designed to help you use ChatGPT in a professional setting.

Who Should Attend:

This course is ideal for office workers who are new to ChatGPT or have only used it for basic question-and-answer interactions. No prior experience with AI is required, making this course suitable for anyone looking to enhance their work skills with the help of AI.

Key Takeaways:

  • Practical experience with ChatGPT for workplace tasks.
  • Improved ability to craft clear prompts for better results.
  • Confidence in using AI to support daily office tasks and boost productivity.
